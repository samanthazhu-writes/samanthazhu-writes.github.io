Creative Practice

Stories built through documents, memory, and participation.

My practice sits between literary writing, interactive narrative design, and archive-based storytelling. I am interested in stories where characters are not only remembered through plot, but through documents, objects, testimony, revised language, and the systems that try to define them.

01

Document-driven narratives

Stories built through letters, case files, evidence fragments, testimonies, reports, diaries, and institutional language.

02

Low-pressure participation

Interactive formats that do not require loud performance or aggressive social play, but invite readers or players to read, choose, classify, remember, and decide.

03

Web as narrative form

Simple web pages, archive interfaces, access gates, and digital logs used not only as promotion, but as part of the storytelling itself.

MAIN PROJECT PORTAL

She Was Not a Goddess: The Adèle Archive

《她不是女神：阿黛勒档案》

A modular archive-fiction narrative world about a female scientist transformed after death into a Mother Goddess by the civilisation that used her body, research, language, and memory.

The project includes a six-player participatory archive trial, live and workshop-friendly companion pieces, fictional institutional documents, digital archive interfaces, and intimate audio / reading fragments.

It is not designed as one fixed script, but as a story-world with multiple possible entry points.

WORK 01

The Night Butterfly Does Not Fall

《夜蝶不落》

An emotional four-player script game about escape, intimacy, being chosen, and the fragile courage to survive.

Set in a European courtly atmosphere of pursuit, secrecy, and emotional judgement, the project focuses on character relationships, companionship, vulnerability, and whether a person can still be loved after being used by power.

In development
Emotional script game 4 players Courtly atmosphere Escape Intimacy

WORK 02

The Raven Valley Covenant

《鸦谷契约》

A Gothic inheritance story about family secrets, old houses, binding promises, and the cost of accepting a legacy that was never fully explained.

The project is built around contracts, forbidden memory, ancestral power, and the atmosphere of a house where every room seems to remember a different version of the past.

In development
Gothic fiction Family mystery Contract narrative Inheritance Memory

WORK 03

Medici Requiem

《美第奇的安魂曲》

A Renaissance political tribunal and emotional mystery about art, patronage, family power, double death, and a city choosing what history is allowed to remember.

Designed for seven players and one facilitator, the project combines historical Gothic atmosphere, multi-party responsibility, evidence-based judgement, and a finale in which players decide not only what happened, but how Florence will write it down.

In development
Historical Gothic 7 players + facilitator Renaissance tribunal Art Judgement

WORK 04

Dark Paradise

《暗黑天堂》

A dark fable of divided identity, desire, control, and the cost of being split between the self that survives and the self performed for others.

The project is currently being restructured as a psychological and symbolic narrative, with emphasis on doubles, performance, hidden violence, and the fragile border between protection and possession.
